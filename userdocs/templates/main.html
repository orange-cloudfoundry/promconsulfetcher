What is Promconsulfetcher ?

flash_on

Retrieve and merge your prometheus from all consul services

This was made for let you retrieve and merge with relabelling all your prometheus metrics from all your consul services when it's not accessible directly by prometheus

group

User Experience Focused

You can retrieve all your app instance without using the broker by simply call endpoint {{.BaseURL}}/v1/services/[consul template style query]/metrics if you have your metrics available on /metrics endpoint on your app.

settings

Easy to work with

You can set your own endpoint by using associated service broker.
